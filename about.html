History

LESS was developed by Alexis Sellier, more commonly known as @cloudhead. The Less.js project is now mantained and extended by the community and the core LESS team.

LESS was created in 2009 by Alexis Sellier, more commonly known as @cloudhead.

Core team

Less.js is maintained by the founding team and a small group of invaluable core contributors, with the massive support and involvement of our community.

Ports of Less.js

LESS has been ported to several other programming languages.

    Java

    • LESS Engine(Runs less.js in the Rhino JVM-based JavaScript interpreter)
    • LESS CSS Compiler for Java(Runs less.js in the Rhino JVM-based JavaScript interpreter)
    • Less4j(Native Java implementation.)

    .Net

    • LESS CSS for .Net

    PHP

    • lessphp
    • BW LESS CSS(WordPress Plugin)

    Ruby

    • Less.js In Ruby's V8 Engine

Have another language to add, or perhaps a different or better translation? Let us know by opening an issue.
